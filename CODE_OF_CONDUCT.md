# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want `findmy-trail` to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone,
regardless of experience, background, or identity.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good faith; critique ideas, not people.
- Keep discussions on-topic and helpful.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing others' private information.
- **Promoting or assisting misuse of this tool to track people without their
  consent.** This project exists to help people locate their *own* devices.
  Advocating stalking, surveillance, or abuse is grounds for an immediate ban.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, commits, issues, and contributions that violate
this code, and may ban repeat or serious offenders. Report concerns privately via
[Security Advisories](https://github.com/FixITSukil/findmy-trail/security/advisories/new)
or a direct message to the maintainer.

Adapted in spirit from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org).
